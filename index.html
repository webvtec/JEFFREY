Who We Are

ABOUT US

LXL Home Improvement LLC is a fully licensed home improvement contractor based in New York City. We serve homeowners across all five boroughs — the Bronx, Queens, Brooklyn, Manhattan, and Staten Island.

Our team of skilled tradespeople specializes in full residential remodels — from carpentry and flooring to complete kitchen and bathroom transformations. We take pride in clean workmanship, honest pricing, and treating every home like our own.

We are available 7 days a week because we know home emergencies and project timelines don't wait for the work week. Whether it's a small repair or a full gut renovation, LXL delivers quality you can see.
